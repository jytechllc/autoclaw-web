# AutoClaw First Orders Playbook

## Objective

Get the first US-market deals for AutoClaw by selling one narrow promise:

`We help B2B teams build outbound pipeline with lead generation, contact enrichment, cold email setup, and follow-up automation.`

Do not lead with:

- AI employees
- all-in-one marketing automation
- SEO
- social media
- generic automation

Lead with:

- sample leads
- outbound setup
- pipeline creation
- fewer manual prospecting hours

## Fastest ICPs

### ICP 1: Founder-led B2B SaaS

- Team size: 10-100
- Sales motion: outbound or founder-led sales
- Need: more meetings without hiring more SDRs
- Offer: `We can stand up your outbound system in 14 days`

### ICP 2: Agencies and service firms

- Team size: 5-50
- Deal size: high-ticket services
- Need: more qualified accounts and more consistent follow-up
- Offer: `We help you find accounts, enrich contacts, and launch outbound sequences`

### ICP 3: Exporters targeting North America

- Team size: 10-200
- Need: more US buyers, distributors, or channel partners
- Offer: `We build the list, contact data, and outbound workflow for your US market push`

## Offer Ladder

### Offer 1: Free sample leads

- Promise: 10-20 sample leads for the prospect's ICP
- CTA: show capability fast

### Offer 2: Outbound setup

- Price target: $1,500-$3,000 setup
- Deliverables:
- ICP definition
- target account list
- enriched contacts
- outbound copy
- workflow configuration

### Offer 3: Managed outbound

- Price target: $3,000-$8,000 per month
- Deliverables:
- recurring list building
- enrichment
- cold email iterations
- follow-up workflow
- weekly reporting

## Seven-Day Execution Plan

### Day 1

- Pick one ICP only
- Start with `founder-led B2B SaaS` or `high-ticket agencies`
- Use the lead list in `us-priority-leads.csv`

### Day 2

- Send 30 personalized cold emails
- Send 20 LinkedIn connection requests
- Use the templates in `outreach-templates.md`

### Day 3

- Send follow-up #1 to non-responders
- Offer free sample leads

### Day 4

- Build 2 sample lead packs for the warmest prospects
- Use the AutoClaw trial flow as proof

### Day 5

- Push for calls
- Goal: 3-5 calls booked

### Day 6

- Convert calls into paid setup proposals
- Keep the proposal simple:
- timeline: 14 days
- milestone: live outbound workflow
- outcome: first qualified pipeline

### Day 7

- Follow up every prospect with a decision ask
- Close the first setup engagement before pushing subscriptions

## Qualification Questions

- Who are you selling to in the US?
- What is your average deal size?
- How are you getting leads today?
- Who owns outbound right now?
- What happens after the first touch?

If they cannot answer these, sell setup first. Do not sell software access first.

## Goal For The First Deal

Do not optimize for MRR first.

Optimize for:

- one paid setup
- one active case study
- one working outbound workflow

That first deal gives you:

- proof
- language
- screenshots
- metrics
- a reference account

## Sources Used For Priority Leads

Priority leads are built from public US-market buying signals, not scraped blindly.

Selection rules:

- the company is actively hiring SDR, BDR, AE, RevOps, or growth roles
- the role description mentions outbound, pipeline, prospecting, sequencing, enrichment, or target accounts
- the team looks like it can buy a small setup engagement quickly
- the company is already selling into the US or explicitly growing North America

How the list is built:

- find target companies from public careers pages and hiring systems
- capture the specific signal in `us-priority-leads.csv`
- use the company domain to enrich decision-maker contacts with Apollo first and Hunter second
- send a short sample-leads offer first, then follow up on Day 2 and Day 5
